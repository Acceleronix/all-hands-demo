Customer Overview

Company Background

NitroGen Industrial Solutions S.p.A is an innovative industrial gas production company based in Milan, Italy, specializing in on-site nitrogen generation systems for manufacturing, food processing, pharmaceutical, and chemical industries across Europe. Founded in 2018.

Target Market

European industrial sector focusing on manufacturers requiring sustainable nitrogen production solutions. Primary markets include automotive manufacturing, food packaging, pharmaceutical production, and chemical processing facilities seeking to reduce costs and environmental impact.

Platform Requirements

Seeking comprehensive IoT platform to transform traditional nitrogen production facilities into digital smart factories. Critical needs include remote control capabilities, real-time monitoring and alerting, energy optimization through renewable integration, and resource conservation tracking for sustainability goals.

Technical Foundation

4G LTE cellular connectivity (Quectel EC200U-EU) embedded in nitrogen generation hardware systems, providing reliable industrial-grade remote access and monitoring with Acceleronix SIM/eSIM connectivity services and PaaS platform integration.
