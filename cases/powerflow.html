Customer Overview

Company Background

SolarPulse India is a Bangalore-based startup founded in 2020, specializing in mobile solar energy storage solutions for urban families and small businesses, supporting India's National Solar Mission 2030 vision to reduce carbon footprint and create additional revenue streams.

Target Market

Urban middle-class families and camping enthusiasts (aged 25-45), distributed energy operators, and small commercial users seeking sustainable, portable solar energy solutions with IoT optimization capabilities.

Product Innovation

SolarPulse PowerHub series (2025) developed with local partner TechVibe, featuring 1-5kWh capacity range, perfect for balcony PV systems and camping applications with intelligent solar energy utilization.

Smart Technology

Built-in WiFi and Bluetooth Low Energy connectivity with SolarPulse App for wireless control, charging scheduling, grid feed-in management, and OTA updates optimized for India's high-temperature climate.
