Customer Overview

Company Background

PowerFlow is India's leading mobile energy storage provider, supplying portable battery solutions to construction companies, event organizers, infrastructure projects, and emergency services across major Indian cities and industrial corridors.

Target Market

Construction and infrastructure projects, outdoor events and festivals, film productions, emergency response teams, remote telecommunications sites, and off-grid industrial applications.

Current Platform

Manual deployment tracking with basic usage meters. Lacks remote monitoring, predictive maintenance, and optimal deployment algorithms. High maintenance costs and unexpected failures.

Technical Foundation

Lithium-ion battery banks with integrated smart controllers. Requires LTE-M/4G connectivity for remote locations across India's diverse geography and comprehensive IoT platform for fleet optimization.
