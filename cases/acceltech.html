Customer Overview

Company Background

AccelTech is an IoT solutions provider based in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, specializing in tracking solutions for cold chain logistics, particularly refrigeration systems in trucks.

Target Market

Southeast Asia logistics and cold chain industry with focus on pharmaceutical and food transportation requiring temperature-controlled environments.

Current Platform

Firebase backend deployed on AWS server with device management capabilities, but lacking OTA functionality for firmware updates.

Technical Foundation

Quectel BG95-M3 module providing NB-IoT connectivity and LBS (Location Based Services) for comprehensive tracking.
