Customer Overview

Company Background

HeatFlow Systems SAS is a smart energy solutions provider based in Paris, France, specializing in IoT-enabled heat pump systems for residential and commercial applications. The company focuses on providing end-to-end solutions including hardware, software, and ongoing maintenance services for the French market. Founded in 2020.

Target Market

French homeowners and property developers seeking smart energy solutions that align with the French government's heat pump incentive programs and net-zero emissions targets by 2050. Focus on households transitioning from gas boilers to sustainable heat pump systems.

Platform Requirements

Seeking comprehensive IoT platform to replace manual data collection processes. Critical needs include professional heat pump management platform, remote monitoring and control, predictive maintenance alerts, voice control integration, automated service scheduling, and condition monitoring to prevent frequent pump failures for 10K device fleet.

Technical Foundation

4G LTE cellular connectivity (Quectel QCI001 4G DTU) embedded in heat pump hardware system, providing reliable remote access and real-time monitoring capabilities with Acceleronix SIM/eSIM connectivity services and PaaS platform integration.
