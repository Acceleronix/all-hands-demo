Customer Overview

Company Background

SmartGlow SIA is a smart lighting solutions provider based in Riga, Latvia, specializing in IoT-enabled intelligent bulbs and fixtures for residential and commercial applications across the Baltic and Nordic regions. Founded in 2021.

Target Market

Baltic and Nordic region smart home market focusing on middle to high-end consumers (aged 28-50) seeking energy-efficient, aesthetically pleasing, and voice-controlled lighting solutions for modern homes and small offices.

Platform Requirements

Seeking comprehensive IoT device management platform to replace basic MQTT broker setup. Critical needs include OTA firmware updates, multi-protocol support (WiFi/BLE), smart home ecosystem integration (HomeKit/Alexa/Google Home), energy consumption monitoring, and scene management for 8K device fleet.

Technical Foundation

WiFi 2.4GHz + Bluetooth Low Energy dual-mode connectivity providing reliable local and cloud control with WebSocket real-time communication and MQTT message queuing for smart home automation.
